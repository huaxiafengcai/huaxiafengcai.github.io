Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the imToken Trace For ETH extension (hereinafter referred to as "this extension"). We highly value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

This extension does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to provide any personal data, such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Information Usage

This extension locally processes and manages your Ethereum transaction information on your device, including transfers, receiving payments, and transaction history. We do not access or transmit this information to our servers or any third party.

3. Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any personal information, this extension will not share, sell, or trade your information with any third party.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your transaction data. This extension uses encryption technology to ensure that your transaction information is processed only on your device and is not disclosed to any external entity.

5. Your Choices

You can stop using this extension at any time. If you choose to uninstall this extension, all data stored on your device will be permanently deleted.

6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically to ensure that you are aware of the latest privacy practices.
